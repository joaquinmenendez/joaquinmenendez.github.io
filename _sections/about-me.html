--- title: About Me icon: fa-user order: 3 ---

I studied and worked as a lecturer and research assistant at the University of Buenos Aires.
During those years, I gained exposure to different fields such as experimental design, behavior analysis, and neuroscience.
Throughout this fascinating journey, I realized that I needed more sophisticated tools to properly address such a complex topic as human behavior.
Eager by this curiosity, I decided to enroll at Duke to improve my abilities in programming, machine learning, and statistical modeling.
I am interested in using data science as a tool to understand and resolve problems in healthcare, and at the same time, to produce a social impact on people's lives.
